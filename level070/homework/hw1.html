Spaghetti Carbonara

Enjoy this simple and creamy pasta dish with a rich combination of eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 50g Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
3. Beat eggs and mix with the cheeses.
4. Combine pasta with pancetta and then toss with the egg mixture.
5. Serve with a sprinkle of black pepper and enjoy!
