At the heart of every Mustang is a high-powered engine. Mustang offers four available engines,
including the 5.2L supercharged V8.

EcoBoost®

  • 2.3L EcoBoost® engine
  • 310 horsepower
  • 350 lb.-ft. of torque*
  • Turbocharged performance
  • Available 2.3L High-Performance Package

GT

  • 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 engine
  • 450 horsepower
  • 410 lb.-ft. of torque*
  • Available with 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic

Mach 1

  • 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 engine
  • 480 horsepower
  • 420 lb.-ft. of torque*
  • Upgraded cooling systems
  • Track-focused performance

Shelby® GT500®

  • 5.2L Supercharged V8
  • 760 horsepower
  • 625 lb.-ft. of torque*
  • Most powerful street-legal Ford engine
